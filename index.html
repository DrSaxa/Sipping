Welcome

This game is all about shared fun and unforgettable moments.
One by one, each person takes on the challenges. You can freely decide whether to choose a reward for the other players after each challenge mastered, introduce a point system, or simply play together with joy. Celebrate the winner or just enjoy the time together. Likewise, you can do each challenge as many times and as long as you wish.

Let your creativity run wild and have a fantastic time!
